Plume is a label dedicated to releasing albums from the Northwest and beyond that we feel compelled to share with you. We see the need to create another platform to release carefully-crafted and imaginative records which otherwise might go overlooked. We hope to build an enigmatic and expansive compendium of music for your listening pleasure.
